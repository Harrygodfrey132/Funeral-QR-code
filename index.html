Creating Digital Legacies

Our journey began with the desire to help families preserve the memories of their loved ones in a lasting and meaningful way. We create custom memorial web pages, accessible through QR codes placed on gravestones. By simply scanning the code, visitors are taken to a dedicated page that celebrates their loved one’s life. Each page is secure with password protection, ensuring that their legacy is shared safely and cherished for generations to come

  • Custom Memorial Web Pages
  • Handmade QR Codes
  • Password Protected for Security
  • Easily Accessible on Any Device

How it works

We work on a 3 step process to ensure your loved one's memorial page is created and shared in a way that is easy and accessible for everyone.

Step 1: Scan the QR Code

By Scanning your personalised QR code, you will be redirected to your loved one's memorial page. Allowing you to set your own customised URL

Step 2: Edit your personalised memorial page

Adding your own personal touch to the memorial page, by adding your own photos, videos, and messages. Give the option for others to upload photos and videos to the memorial page.

Step 3: Share your memorial page

Place the QR code in a discrete area on the gravestone or in the funeral home. Allowing family and friends to easily access the memorial page.
